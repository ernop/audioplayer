San Francisco Science Fiction Short Story Reading Group

What

We get together Tuesdays to discuss Science Fiction stories. We have also written our own stories and had experimental art AI parties and coding events, too. To join: check the Schedule and Story list below & read the next story, then come to the meeting to talk about it and have fun! Meetings are free and informal; you can come when you want, or skip weeks you don't like the story or are busy. Also: talk about futurism, AI art, AIs, debate the meaning of sci-fi and where it fits within literature.

When and Where

Every week on Tuesday Evenings, 7:00 pm California Time, USA. Location: Ernie's House near College of San Mateo. Join the discord for the address, or email ernestfrench@gmail.com. The QRCode is the discord invite ===>

Is this group for you?

Yes! If you are reading this page, you are welcome! If you think that you need to learn more before coming, well, I'm saying you're invited right now and you would probably have fun if you came by! It'll be fun! We have people who have read a lot of scifi, and those who only ever read it for the group. That's okay. We just want to talk about ideas and ride this crazy world into the future!
